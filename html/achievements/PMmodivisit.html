PM Modi Visits AKGEC Skills Stall

Previous Next
16DEC2018

It is a matter of pride for AKGEC that Hon’able Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi visited AKGEC Skills Foundation (ASF) Stall during visit to Modern Coach Factory (MCF) at Rae Bareli on 16th December 2018. He appreciated the joint efforts of ASF & MCF to conduct First In Plant Robotic Training Program for technical professionals of MCF and other coach factories of Indian Railways. AKGEC team briefed the Prime Minister on the joint activities conducted by ASF and MCF in recent past in their endeavor to support ‘Make in India’ and ‘Skill India’ initiatives of Govt. of India, These included ‘Training Program on Smart Manufacturing’ conducted at AKGEC Campus for ten young officers of Indian Railways during 17th July’18 to 31st July’ 18 and a one day workshop on “Manufacturing Execution System” conducted at MCF plant for senior and middle management of MCF. These are now being followed up with regular inplant training programs conducted by AKGEC at MCF, Rae Bareli.
