Bachelor of Technology in

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Electrical Engineering stream involve the study and applications of Electricity, Electronics and Electromagnetics that entails and encompasses Power Engineering, Control Systems, Signal Processing, Digital Computers and Instrumentation. The department is running undergraduate course in Electrical & Electronics Engineering since 2003 with a current intake of 120. Prior to this the course was Electrical Engineering since the inception of the college in 1998. The department is also running Post Graduate course on Electrical Power and Energy System since 2009 with the intake of 18.Now this PG course is renewed as Electrical & Electronics Engineering in 2016.

HOD

Dr. Bhupal Singh

Dr. Bhupal Singh has teaching and research experience of over 25 years. He has worked as JRF at IET Lucknow (CSIR project on Solar Photo Voltaic) for over 3 years and teaching assistant for approximately 0.5 years in Electrical Engineering Department of MNNIT Allahabad after M.E in 1997. He was senior research fellow at IIT Delhi for over 5 years during his doctorate. He has served AKGEC as Assistant Professor (2007-08), Associate Professor (2008-09) and Professor since 2009. He was also HOD, EN during 2009-10 period. He has 7 national and international publications to his credit. He has guided over 35 B. Tech & M. Tech Project and guiding PhD students also. His area of interest includes Image Processing, Bio-Medical Instrumentation and Signal Processing.
