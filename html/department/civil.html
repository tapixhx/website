Bachelor of Technology in

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is the art of directing the sources of power in nature for the use and convenience of man, for improvement of quality of life. The theory and practice of Civil Engineering is basically applied in the construction of buildings & structures, roads, bridges, aqueducts, canals, river navigation ports, docks and harbours.At AKGEC, B.Tech course in Civil Engineering is offered with intake capacity of 120.The goal of the department is to produce high quality technical manpower needed by industry, R&D organizations and academic institutions and above all to meet the engineering needs of the nation.
